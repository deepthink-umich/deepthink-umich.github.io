Principal Investigator

Qing Qu

Assistant Professor

Electrical and Computer Engineering

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor

Dr. Qing Qu leads the DeepThink Lab with a focus on computational methods for learning low-complexity models from high-dimensional data. His research spans the foundations of generative AI, deep representation learning, and machine learning for scientific applications.
